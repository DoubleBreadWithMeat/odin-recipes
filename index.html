Guides for Gaffs

Below, with great pleasure, do we have a swell selection of brief: recipes, guides, and instructions; aimed expertly with the utmost in mild misdirection; to tickle fancies and elicit only the most moderate of chuckles. We ask, dear reader, only that as your chest doth jump, but only once perchance if at all, and a life giving breath does make haste in its exodus from your lungs up through your nostrils at oh such an elevated rate, that you do politely bear in mind that this all is naught but a humble, and in intention simple, exercise at the start of a much larger curriculum, with an unbalanced time investment to mastery ratio and, no real need for any of it to be in such a way as it is. That is to say, this project, in essence, meets loosely only some of the criteria from which it was created, while simultaneously taking more time to complete than anything considerable as having even half of its technical accomplishments. A waste of what could have otherwise been productive time really, and about that there really is nothing all that funny.
